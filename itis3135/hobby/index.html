What is Planking? 🤔

Planking is lying face down in absurd public places, taking a photo, and posting it online for clout. You just had to be there to get it.

Image credits: Donkey100 CC BY-SA 3.0

Who Planks? 😎

Anyone with a sense of humor and zero shame! Famous plankers include:

  • College students avoiding homework
  • Office workers rebelling against monotony
  • Your weird cousin

When to Plank? 🕒

Planking is best done:

  • During awkward family gatherings
  • When your boss isn’t looking
  • At 3 AM for no reason

How to Plank? 📜

Step-by-step guide:

  2. Lie face down, stiff as a board
  3. Have a friend snap a pic
  4. Post with #Planking
